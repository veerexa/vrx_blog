🛰️Pixxel Space

While companies like Skyroot and Agnikul build the delivery trucks to orbit, Pixxel builds the cargo. Pixxel is assembling the world’s most advanced constellation of hyperspectral earth imaging satellites.

Seeing the Unseen: Hyperspectral Magic

Traditional satellites capture images in standard visual spectrums (RGB). Hyperspectral imaging captures data across hundreds of narrow spectral bands. This allows Pixxel's satellites to detect chemical compositions from orbit.

Real-World Applications:

  • Agriculture: Detecting unseen crop diseases, identifying exact soil nutrient composition, and predicting yields weeks before visual symptoms manifest.
  • Climate & Environment: Tracking invisible methane leaks, measuring ocean pollution, and monitoring the exact health of forests.
  • Mining & Energy: Identifying underground mineral deposits and rare earth elements without drilling.

Constellation Launch

Pixxel has already successfully launched its tech demonstration satellites (Shakuntala and Anand) utilizing SpaceX and ISRO launch vehicles. They are actively expanding to a full 24-satellite constellation that will provide highly frequent planetary imaging.

📚 Core Metrics

  • Founders Awais Ahmed, Kshitij Khandelwal
  • Headquarters Bengaluru, India & Los Angeles, USA
  • Key Tech Hyperspectral Imaging Satellites, AI Data Analytics, Earth Observation
