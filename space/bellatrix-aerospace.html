💧Bellatrix Aerospace

Bellatrix Aerospace focuses on what happens after a rocket reaches orbit. They specialize in in-space propulsion systems designed to make satellites highly maneuverable, efficient, and exceptionally eco-friendly.

Water as Fuel: The Microwave Plasma Thruster

One of Bellatrix's most globally significant innovations is their Microwave Plasma Thruster (MPT). Instead of using highly toxic, expensive chemical propellants like Hydrazine, the MPT literally uses water. The system converts liquid water into plasma using microwave energy to generate thrust. This drastically reduces mission costs, simplifies fueling protocols, and eliminates toxicity warnings.

Major Breakthroughs:

  • Orbital Transfer Vehicles (OTVs): Bellatrix is developing space "taxis" (OTVs) capable of receiving satellites in low orbit and delivering them to precise higher orbits.
  • Green Propulsion Variants: Beyond water, they maintain a portfolio of advanced High-Performance Green Propellants (HPGP).
  • ISRO Collaboration: Bellatrix received massive validation when they were contracted by ISRO to develop Hall-effect thrusters.

Setting New Standards

As the space sector shifts rapidly towards sustainable operations, Bellatrix's zero-toxicity solutions are capturing global interest, positioning them as a cornerstone of the future satellite manufacturing supply chain.
