🚀Skyroot Aerospace

Skyroot Aerospace is widely considered the pioneer of India's private space launch sector. Founded by former ISRO scientists, the company is building a family of highly efficient, low-cost orbital launch vehicles.

Massive Milestones: The Prarambh Mission

In November 2022, Skyroot achieved an unprecedented milestone by launching the Vikram-S rocket, India's first privately developed launch vehicle. The suborbital flight successfully demonstrated the viability of their propulsion technology and composite material architecture.

What they do best:

  • Vikram Series: A trio of launch vehicles (Vikram I, II, III) capable of carrying payloads ranging from 290 kg to 815 kg to Low Earth Orbit.
  • Dhawan-1 Cryogenic Engine: India's first privately developed, fully 3D printed cryogenic engine, fueled by LNG and Liquid Oxygen (LOX).
  • Carbon Composites: They use advanced carbon composite structures that make the rockets dramatically lighter than traditional metal architectures.

The Future is Reusable

Moving beyond expendable rockets, Skyroot is actively researching reusable vehicle technology to further democratize space access and lower the cost per kilogram of payload to orbit, ensuring India remains incredibly competitive globally.

📚 Core Metrics

  • Founders Pawan Kumar Chandana, Naga Bharath Daka
  • Headquarters Hyderabad, India
  • Key Tech Carbon-composite rockets, 3D printed Cryogenic Engines, Vikram Launch Vehicles
