How to Become a Doctor in India (Step-by-Step 2026)

Your ultimate guide to medical professions, from NEET preparation to choosing between MBBS and BDS.

1. The Roadmap to MBBS

Becoming a doctor in India is a journey of dedication. The primary step is clearing the NEET-UG entrance exam.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB).
  • Entrance: NEET-UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test).
  • Duration: 5.5 years (including 1 year internship).

2. MBBS vs BDS: Which is Better?

While MBBS focuses on general medicine and surgery, BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) is dedicated to oral healthcare.

Feature MBBS BDS
Focus Whole body medicine Dental & Oral health
Duration 5.5 Years 5 Years
NEET Score Generally higher Moderate to High

3. NEET Preparation Strategy

Consistent practice is key to cracking NEET. Focus on NCERT books as they form the core of the syllabus.

  • Biology: Pure NCERT lines (90% questions).
  • Chemistry: Organic and Inorganic from NCERT; Physical for numericals.
  • Physics: Numerical practice and formula sheets.

4. Other Medical Careers

Medicine isn't just about BEING a doctor. Consider these vital roles:

  • Nursing: The backbone of healthcare.
  • Physiotherapy: Rehabilitation and movement specialists.
  • Pharmacy: Experts in drug chemistry and distribution.
