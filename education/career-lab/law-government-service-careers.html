Law & Government Service Careers: A Roadmap

Service to the nation through administration and legal excellence. Learn how to crack UPSC and legal entrances.

1. Becoming an IAS Officer: The UPSC Journey

The Civil Services Examination (CSE) conducted by **UPSC** is the gateway to the Indian Administrative Service (IAS).

  • Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline from a recognized university.
  • Stages: Prelims (MCQs), Mains (Descriptive), and the Interview.
  • Focus: General studies, current affairs, and an optional subject expertise.

2. Legal Careers: Lawyer to Judge

Law is a prestigious profession with diverse paths in corporate law, litigation, and the judiciary.

  • Entrance: CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) for National Law Universities.
  • Degrees: 5-year integrated B.A./B.B.A. LL.B. or a 3-year LL.B. after graduation.
  • Career Path: Practice in courts, join a Law Firm, or clear the Judiciary exams to become a Judge.

3. Top Govt Jobs After Graduation

Beyond IAS, several other government sectors offer stable and rewarding careers:

  • SSC CGL: Staff Selection Commission for various central govt departments.
  • Banking: IBPS PO and SBI PO exams for officer roles in public sector banks.
  • Defense: CDS (Combined Defence Services) for Army, Navy, and Air Force.

4. Public Policy Analyst

A new-age govt-linked role where professionals analyze data to help design better national and state policies. Requires deep understanding of social sciences and data analysis.
