How School ERP Software Helps Schools Run More Efficiently

Updated: March 2026 7 min read

Managing a school is a complex challenge. From tracking student records to collecting fees and communicating with parents, administrators juggle dozens of responsibilities. School ERP software solves this by integrating all functions into one smart, automated platform.

Schools Using ERP Report:

  • • 60% reduction in administrative workload
  • • 40% faster fee collection rates
  • • 85% improvement in parent satisfaction
  • • 90% fewer data entry errors

Key Ways School ERP Helps Schools

1. Streamlining Admissions

With a school ERP, the entire admission workflow becomes digital. Parents submit applications online, upload documents, and track status in real time. Administrators get an organized dashboard of all applications and confirmations.

2. Automating Fee Collection

Veerexa integrates with payment gateways to accept fees via UPI, net banking, or cards. Automated reminders are sent before due dates, reducing defaults significantly and eliminating manual cash handling.

3. Simplifying Attendance Tracking

Teachers mark attendance digitally via mobile app in seconds. Parents receive automatic SMS alerts when their child is absent — improving both accountability and student safety.

"The digital report card generation alone saved our teachers two weeks of work at the end of every term."
