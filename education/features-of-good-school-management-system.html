10 Must-Have Features of a Good School Management System

Not all school management software is created equal. Before investing in a school ERP system, you need to know exactly what features to look for. This checklist covers the 10 non-negotiable features that every school management system in India must have.

Exclusive Feature Set

✅ Online admissions
✅ UPI Fee collection
✅ Parent SMS alerts
✅ Automatic Reports

1. Admission Management

A good school management system must digitize the entire admission process — from online application submission and document upload to fee payment and enrollment confirmation.

2. Online Fee Collection with UPI Support

Fee collection is the financial backbone of any school. Your school software must integrate with Indian payment gateways supporting UPI, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
