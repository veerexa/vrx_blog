Innovation for National Security: The DRDO Story

Empowering India with cutting-edge defense technologies, from supersonic cruise missiles to state-of-the-art combat platforms.

50+ Labs Across India
1958 Inception Year

The Mission: Strategic Autonomy

Formed in 1958 by merging the Defence Science Organisation (DSO) and others, **DRDO**'s primary goal is to design and develop state-of-the-art sensors, weapon systems, and platforms for the Indian Armed Forces.

🚀

Missile Systems

IGMDP led by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam revolutionized India's strike capability.

🛡️

Electronic Warfare

Development of advanced radars (NETRA) and communication systems.

Key Pillars

A Network of Specialized Laboratories

ADE
Bengaluru

Specializing in Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and aeronautical systems.

DRDL
Hyderabad

The nerve center for missile research and strategic development.

CVRDE
Chennai

Pioneers in combat vehicle technology, including the Arjun MBT.

ARDE
Pune

Innovation center for small arms and advanced armament systems.

Legacy of Breakthroughs

DRDO's innovations form the backbone of India's indigenous defense manufacturing.

Strategic

Agni Missiles

A family of long-range ballistic missiles capable of carrying nuclear warheads.

Aeronautics

Worlds smallest and lightest multi-role supersonic fighter aircraft.

Armour

Superior firepower, high mobility, and excellent protection in a single platform.
