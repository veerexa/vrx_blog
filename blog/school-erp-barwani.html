Barwani district in western Madhya Pradesh is home to hundreds of schools serving tens of thousands of students. From the district headquarters in Barwani town to remote tehsil schools in Pansemal and Anjad, every school faces the same challenge: managing operations with limited staff and outdated manual systems.

Veerexa was built for exactly this environment — with offline capability, Hindi interface, MP State Board compliance, and pricing starting at ₹999/month.

  • Fully offline-capable — works without internet, syncs automatically
  • Complete Hindi interface for teachers and staff
  • MP State Board format report cards built-in
  • Covers: Barwani, Rajpur, Sendhwa, Anjad, Pansemal, Thikri
  • Starting at ₹999/month — no hidden fees

Challenges Specific to Barwani District Schools

  • Intermittent internet connectivity in rural areas — cloud-only software is impractical
  • Small admin teams — teachers handle both teaching and administrative duties
  • Parent communication across remote villages requires SMS, not apps alone
  • Manual register-keeping creates gaps during harvest season when staff are stretched

How Veerexa Solves These Problems

📶 Works Offline — Always

Attendance, exams, fees — all work without internet. Data syncs to the cloud when connectivity returns. Schools in Sendhwa and Pansemal use this feature daily.

🗣️ Full Hindi Language Support

The entire platform, SMS alerts, and parent communications are available in Hindi — making adoption natural for Barwani's schools.

📋 MP State Board Compliance

Report cards, mark sheets, and academic records in exact MP State Board format — generated in minutes, not days.

💳 UPI Fees Collection

UPI adoption has reached even rural Barwani. Veerexa lets parents pay via PhonePe or Google Pay, eliminating cash handling risks.

📲 SMS Alerts for All Parents

Parents without smartphones still receive attendance and fee alerts via SMS — ensuring no family is left out.
